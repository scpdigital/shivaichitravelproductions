About Shivai Chitravel Productions

Since 2014, Shivai Chitravel Productions has been a creative powerhouse dedicated to transforming ideas into captivating visual stories. Specializing in photography and videography, we have spent nearly a decade capturing timeless moments—from weddings and pre-weddings to corporate events, brand launches, hospital campaigns, and influencer promotions.

In 2025, we expanded our creative horizons by launching our digital marketing division, offering social media management, content creation, and growth strategies for actors, brands, health care, travel adventures and businesses.

Our team combines artistry, technology, and strategic thinking to craft experiences that inspire, connect, and leave a lasting impact. We don’t just capture moments—we create narratives that reflect emotions, elevate brands, and engage audiences across platforms.

With a passion for innovation and an eye for detail, we ensure every frame, video, and campaign speaks volumes, helping our clients shine brighter and stand apart in today’s competitive world.
